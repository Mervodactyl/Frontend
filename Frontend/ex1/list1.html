Attributes of the 4 Nations

Here is a reference source regarding the four nations of the Avatar nation. They include both their elements, properties, famous benders and associated creatures.

ELEMENT

FIRE

  • Lives: Archipeligo Islands
  • Creature: Dragon
  • Special Ability: Lightening
  • Colour: Red/ Orange
  • Main character: Zuko

Natives Traits:

  1. Honourable
  2. Strong Willpower
  3. Military Prowess
  4. Equality

ELEMENT

AIR

  • Lives: Mountians
  • Creature: Flying Bison
  • Special Ability: Flight
  • Colour: White/ Gray
  • Main character: Ang

Natives Traits:

  1. Nomadic Lifestyle
  2. Traditionlism
  3. Peaceful Beliefs
  4. Defensive Tactics

ELEMENT

WATER

  • Lives: North/ South Poles
  • Creature: Fish
  • Special Ability: Blood Bending
  • Colour: Blue/ White
  • Main character: Katara

Natives Traits:

  1. Versatility
  2. Community
  3. Creativity
  4. Food

ELEMENT

EARTH

  • Lives: Continent
  • Creature: Badger-moles
  • Special Ability: Metal plus Lava Bending
  • Colour: Green/ Brown
  • Main character: Ang

Natives Traits:

  1. Endurance
  2. Monarchy
  3. Pride
  4. Strength
